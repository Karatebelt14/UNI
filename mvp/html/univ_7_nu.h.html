About Us

National University (NU) has a proud history dating back to its founding in 1900 as Colegio Filipino. Renamed National University in 1930, it has been committed to providing quality education that meets the changing needs of society. Throughout its history, NU has produced graduates who excel in various fields, contributing to national development. Today, NU continues its legacy of academic excellence, innovation, and service as it prepares students to become competent professionals and responsible global citizens.

National University (NU) takes pride in its commitment to providing quality education that empowers students to excel and contribute to society. Established in 1900, NU has been a leader in higher education, offering innovative programs and rigorous academic standards. With a focus on excellence, diversity, and inclusivity, NU fosters a strong sense of pride among its community members. As NU continues to evolve, its legacy of academic excellence remains a source of pride for all.."

MISSION

STUDENTS, by molding them into life-long learners, ethical and spiritual citizens and self-directed agents of change;
