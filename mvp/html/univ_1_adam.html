About Us

Adamson University, nestled in the heart of Manila, Philippines, stands as a historical beacon of education and excellence. Founded in 1932 by Greek chemist Dr. George Lucas Adamson, this prestigious institution has evolved from a small school into a comprehensive university renowned for its rigorous academic programs and vibrant campus life. Adamson University, affectionately known as AdU, is a private educational entity that has been a cornerstone in shaping the minds and futures of countless students across various disciplines.

The university takes pride in offering a wide array of programs in science and technology, engineering, business, education, and the liberal arts, among others, all designed to equip students with the knowledge and skills necessary to excel in a rapidly changing world. Its commitment to producing competent and ethical professionals is evident in its accreditation status and numerous recognitions from educational bodies. Beyond academics, Adamson University is also celebrated for its contributions to research, community service, and cultural development, fostering an environment that encourages students to engage in holistic growth.

MISSION

As a Catholic University, we diligently pursue truth and knowledge, inspired by Gospel values and guided by the teachings of the Church;
As a Vincentian community, we inspire others to follow the example of St. Vincent de Paul , who led and organized his contemporaries in creatively responding to those who are in need;
As an institution of learning, we assist in the formation of competent, creative and socially responsible leaders through our commitment to excellence in discovery, learning and service;
As a catalyst of social transformation, we provide quality services that empower others to become agents of change.

VISION

Adamson University, a Catholic, Vincentian educational institution, is a recognized leading center for quality education particularly for the socially disadvantaged.
