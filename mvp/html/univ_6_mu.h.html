About Us

Mapúa University, one of the Philippines' premier engineering and technological institutions, has a rich and storied history dating back to its establishment in 1925. It was founded by Don Tomas Mapúa, an esteemed architect and engineer, with a vision to provide high-quality education in engineering and architecture. Initially known as the Mapúa Institute of Technology, the institution quickly gained recognition for its rigorous academic programs and emphasis on practical, hands-on learning.

The pride of Mapúa University stems from its distinguished reputation as a leading institution in engineering, architecture, and technology education in the Philippines. Established in 1925 by renowned architect and engineer Don Tomas Mapúa, the university has a legacy of excellence, innovation, and industry relevance. Mapúa's commitment to providing quality education, fostering creativity, and nurturing critical thinking skills empowers its graduates to excel in their chosen fields and contribute meaningfully to society.
