About Us

Lyceum of the Philippines University, founded by Dr. Jose P. Laurel, a renowned scholar and statesman, stands as a beacon of academic excellence. Dr. Laurel's dedication to education, fueled by his illustrious background and commitment to teaching, led to the university's inception in 1952. Despite the challenges of World War II, Dr. Laurel prioritized educational upliftment, laying the foundation for Lyceum's mission to become a premier institution in the Philippines and the Far East.

The pride of Lyceum of the Philippines University (LPU) lies in its legacy of academic excellence, innovation, and societal impact. Established by Dr. Jose P. Laurel, a distinguished scholar and statesman, LPU has upheld a commitment to quality education since its founding in 1952. With a focus on holistic development and cutting-edge research, LPU nurtures students to become competent professionals and compassionate leaders. As a beacon of opportunity and progress, LPU instills in its graduates a sense of pride in their alma mater and a dedication to making meaningful contributions to society.

MISSION

Advance and preserve knowledge by undertaking research and disseminating and utilizing the results;
Provide necessary knowledge and skills to meet entrepreneurial development and the managerial requirements of the industry;
Provide equitable access to learning through relevant, innovative, industry-based and environment-conscious programs and services in the context of nationalism and internationalism.
Establish local and international linkages that will be the source of learning and growth of the members of the academic community.

VISION

An internationally accredited university dedicated to innovation and excellence in the service of God and country.
