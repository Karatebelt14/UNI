About Us

De La Salle University, founded in 1911 by the Brothers of the Christian Schools, positions itself as a leader in molding human resources for the church and the nation. Renowned for academic excellence, prolific research, and community service, it serves as a hub for higher education training. Nestled in Manila, De La Salle University welcomes local and international students seeking quality education. Situated in a vibrant city, it embraces culture and diversity, offering a dynamic learning environment for a brighter future.

The pride of De La Salle University (DLSU) stems from its commitment to academic excellence, community service, and holistic student development. Founded in 1911 by the Brothers of the Christian Schools, DLSU is renowned for its rigorous academic programs, innovative research, and extensive community engagement. With a diverse student body and vibrant campus culture, DLSU fosters leadership, innovation, and social responsibility. Rooted in Catholic values, DLSU instills in its students a sense of purpose and a commitment to making a positive impact in society."

MISSION

Provide quality education rooted in the Lasallian tradition;
Foster holistic student development, critical thinking, and social responsibility;
Produce graduates who are competent professionals, compassionate leaders, and active contributors to nation-building and global development.

VISION

A leading learner-centered and research University bridging faith and scholarship, attuned to a sustainable Earth, and in the service of Church and society, especially the poor and marginalized.
